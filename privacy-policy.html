Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2024

Introduction

Cricket Manager ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only

Cricket Manager stores all data locally on your device, including:

  • Team names and player information
  • Match scores and statistics
  • Player performance data
  • Match history

No Personal Information

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts
  • Any personally identifiable information

How We Use Information

All data you enter into Cricket Manager remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display match scores and statistics
  • Track player performance
  • Maintain match history
  • Generate analytics and visualizations

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using browser local storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud backup or synchronization occurs
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

  • Purpose: Reserved for potential future online features
  • Current Use: Not actively used in the current version
  • Data Transmitted: None

These permissions are declared but not currently utilized for data collection or transmission.

Third-Party Services

Cricket Manager does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party tracking
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud services

Children's Privacy

Cricket Manager does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed to be used by cricket scorers and enthusiasts of all ages. Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Data Security

  • All data remains on your device
  • No transmission of data occurs
  • No server-side storage exists
  • Standard Android security measures protect locally stored data

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Clear data through app settings or by uninstalling
  • Export: Match data can be viewed and recorded manually
  • Control: Complete ownership of all entered data

Changes to Data Handling

If we introduce features that collect or transmit data in future versions:

  • We will update this privacy policy
  • We will notify users through app update notes
  • User consent will be requested where required

Data Retention

  • Data persists on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • No data is retained on external servers
  • No automatic deletion occurs

International Users

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer issues. The app operates entirely on your local device regardless of your location.
